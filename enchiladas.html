Chicken Enchiladas

Description : Enchiladas are a popular Mexican dish that bursts with flavor and comfort in every bite. Made by rolling soft corn tortillas around a variety of fillings such as seasoned meat, beans, cheese, or vegetables, and then baking them in a savory sauce, enchiladas offer a delightful combination of textures and tastes. The rich, tangy enchilada sauce, often made with tomatoes, chilies, and spices, perfectly complements the hearty fillings. Topped with melted cheese and fresh garnishes like cilantro, sour cream, and avocado, enchiladas are a versatile and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed for any occasion, bringing a taste of Mexico to your table.


Ingredients

  • Chicken Mixture :
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups enchilada sauce
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cheese Mixture:
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • Other:
  • 10 flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup sour cream (for topping)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook chicken mixture: Sauté onion and garlic until translucent. Add shredded chicken, enchilada sauce, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Prepare cheese mixture: Mix cheddar cheese and Monterey Jack cheese.
  4. Assemble enchiladas:
    • Spread a thin layer of chicken mixture on a tortilla.
    • Sprinkle some cheese mixture on top.
    • Roll up the tortilla and place it in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    • Repeat with remaining tortillas and chicken mixture.
    • Top with any remaining chicken mixture and cheese mixture.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes.
  6. Rest and serve: Let stand for 10 minutes before serving. Top with sour cream and garnish with chopped cilantro.
