Lasagna

Description : Lasagna is a classic Italian dish renowned for its layers of rich, hearty flavors and comforting textures. Traditionally made with sheets of pasta, it features alternating layers of savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta or béchamel sauce, and gooey melted cheese, all baked to perfection. Whether you prefer a traditional meat lasagna, a vegetarian version packed with fresh vegetables, or a decadent cheese-filled variety, this dish is a crowd-pleaser that warms the heart and satisfies the soul. Each bite offers a perfect blend of flavors and textures, making lasagna a beloved favorite for family dinners and special occasions alike.


Ingredients

  • Chicken Mixture :
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cheese Mixture:
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Other:
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (for topping)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook chicken mixture: Sauté onion and garlic until translucent. Add shredded chicken, marinara sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Cook noodles: Boil lasagna noodles until al dente.
  4. Prepare cheese mixture: Mix ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, and half of the mozzarell
  5. Assemble lasagna:
    • Spread a thin layer of chicken mixture in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    • Layer with noodles, cheese mixture, and chicken mixture.
    • Repeat layers, ending with chicken mixture.
    • Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  7. Rest and serve: Let stand for 15 minutes before serving
