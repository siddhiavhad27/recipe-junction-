Shahi Paneer

Description : Shahi Paneer is a luxurious Indian dish fit for royalty, featuring paneer (Indian cottage cheese) simmered in a creamy and aromatic sauce. Prepared with a rich blend of tomatoes, cashews, cream, and fragrant spices like cardamom and saffron, Shahi Paneer offers a delightful balance of sweetness and spice. This regal dish is garnished with nuts and dried fruits, adding a touch of elegance and flavor to every creamy bite, making it a beloved choice for special occasions and celebrations.


Ingredients

  • Main Ingredients:
  • 200 grams paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts, soaked and blended to a paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter) or oil
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee: In a large pan, heat ghee over medium heat.
  2. Sauté onions: Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste: Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add spices: Mix in turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for another minute.
  5. Add tomatoes: Stir in tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  6. Add cashew paste: Mix in the cashew nut paste and cook for a few minutes.
  7. Add paneer: Add the paneer cubes and gently mix to coat them with the gravy.
  8. Add cream: Stir in fresh cream and garam masala. Simmer for 5-7 minutes on low heat.
  9. Add kasuri methi: Crush dried fenugreek leaves between your palms and add to the curry. Mix well.
  10. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
